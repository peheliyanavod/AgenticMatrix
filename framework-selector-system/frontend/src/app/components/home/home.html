How The System Works

Our methodology combines Large Language Models with a rigorous Weighted Sum Model to eliminate guesswork from your architecture decisions.

1

Natural Language Input

Describe your ideal system, use cases, and constraints in plain text. No complex forms or technical jargon required.

2

AI Weight Extraction

The Gemini AI model interprets your prompt, extracting your true priorities and assigning normalized mathematical weights to 6 core evaluation criteria.

3

Multi-Criteria Scoring

Your dynamic weights are computed against static, evidence-based scores from 10 major agentic frameworks to generate a mathematically ranked list of recommendations.
