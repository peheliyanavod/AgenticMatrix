How The System Works

Our methodology combines AI-driven natural language interpretation with a rigorous Weighted Sum Model against a standardized evaluation matrix.

1

Natural Language Input

Describe your ideal system, specific constraints, and use cases in plain text. The system interprets requirements without complex technical forms.

2

AI Weight Extraction

The Gemini AI model analyzes your prompt to assign normalized mathematical weights across 12 key evaluation criteria, perfectly matching your priorities.

3

Multi-Criteria Scoring

Your dynamic weights are computed against static, evidence-based scores from benchmarked frameworks to generate a transparent, mathematically ranked list.
